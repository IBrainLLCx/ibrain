Our story

IBrain Technology was founded by two friends from Ecuador who met in high school at a military school. They were passionate about technology and the future of the world, and were determined to create a company that would bring the future to homes through the design of smart houses and the development of personalized software for small businesses.


After completing their education, the founders moved to the United States with the goal of pursuing their professional development and careers in the technology industry. They worked hard to build their skills and knowledge, and eventually decided to start their own company, IBrain Technology.


Despite facing many challenges along the way, the founders of IBrain Technology have remained dedicated to their vision of creating a better future through technology. Their hard work and determination have paid off, as the company has grown into a successful and respected business in the tech industry.
